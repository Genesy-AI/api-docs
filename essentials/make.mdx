## Overview

Make (formerly Integromat) is a powerful visual automation platform that allows you to connect Genesy with thousands of other apps and services. With Make, you can create sophisticated workflows and automate complex processes without writing code.

## Availability

<Note>
  The Genesy Make integration is currently available upon request. Please contact our support team to get access to the integration.
</Note>

## What You Can Do

With the Genesy Make integration, you can:

- **Trigger workflows** based on events in Genesy
- **Perform actions** in Genesy from other applications
- **Sync data** between Genesy and your other tools
- **Build complex automation scenarios** with conditional logic and multiple steps

## Getting Started

To request access to the Genesy Make integration:

1. Contact our support team or reach out to your account manager
2. Once approved, you'll receive access to the Genesy app in Make
3. Connect your Genesy account using your API credentials
4. Start building your automation scenarios

## Need Help?

If you have questions about the Make integration or want to discuss your automation use cases, please reach out to our support team.
